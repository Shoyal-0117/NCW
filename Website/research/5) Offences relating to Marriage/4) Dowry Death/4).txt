

**Offence of Dowry Death (Section 80, BNS)**

The offence of 'dowry death' is defined under Section 80 of the Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita, 2023.

**Essential Ingredients:**

For this offence to be established, the following conditions must be proven:

1.  The death of a woman must be caused by burns or bodily injury.
2.  The death must occur under circumstances that are not normal.
3.  The death must occur within seven years of her marriage.
4.  It must be shown that soon before her death, she was subjected to cruelty or harassment by her husband or any relative of her husband.
5.  This cruelty or harassment must have been in connection with a demand for dowry.
