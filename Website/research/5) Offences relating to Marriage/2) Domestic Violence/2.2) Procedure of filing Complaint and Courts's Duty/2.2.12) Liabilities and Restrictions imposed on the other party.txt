

**Obligations of the Respondent**

A respondent against whom orders are passed under the Domestic Violence Act is legally bound to comply with the following:

1.  **Comply with Restrictions:** The respondent must adhere to all restrictions specified in the Protection Order and the Residence Order issued by the court.

2.  **Provide Financial Support:** The respondent can be held accountable for providing monetary relief for the woman and her children, and must pay the compensatory damages as directed by the court in the compensation order.

3.  **Follow Custody Arrangements:** The respondent is required to follow the child custody arrangements determined by the court, as specified in the Custody Order.
