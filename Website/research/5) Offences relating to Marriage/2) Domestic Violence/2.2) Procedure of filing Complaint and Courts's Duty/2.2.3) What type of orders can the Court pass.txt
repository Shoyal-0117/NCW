i. Direct that the women shall not be evicted or excluded from the household or any part of it.

ii. Upon finding the complaint to be genuine, the Magistrate may, direct the Husband or the Woman, to undergo counselling.

iii. Pass a protection order, providing protection to the women which shall remain in force till the Woman applies for discharge.

iv. Grant monetary relief to meet the expenses incurred and losses suffered by the Woman and any child of the Woman due to domestic violence.

v. Grant custody orders of any child or children of Woman.

vi. Compensation/damages for the injuries including mental torture and emotional distress caused by domestic violence. If upon receipt of an application from a Woman, the Magistrate is satisfied that the circumstances so require, he may alter, modify, or revoke an order after recording the reasons in writing.
