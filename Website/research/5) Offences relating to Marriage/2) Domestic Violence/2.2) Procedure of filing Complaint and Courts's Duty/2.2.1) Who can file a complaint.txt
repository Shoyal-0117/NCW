

**Filing a Complaint and Redressal Authorities**

**Who Can File a Complaint:**

A complaint regarding domestic violence can be filed by:
*   The aggrieved woman herself.
*   A parent or any other relative of the aggrieved woman.
*   Any recognized welfare organization or institution on her behalf.

**Authorities for Redressal:**

A complaint can be filed with any of the following authorities to seek relief:
*   A Police Officer.
*   A Protection Officer appointed under the Act.
*   A Service Provider (an organization registered to offer help to victims).
*   Legal aid services and other community-based services.
*   The District Magistrate or a court.
