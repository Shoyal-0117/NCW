
**Definition of Domestic Violence (Section 3)**

The Protection of Women from Domestic Violence Act defines domestic violence to include the following acts:

1.  Any act that harms, injures, or endangers the health, safety, life, limb, or overall well-being (whether mental or physical) of a woman. This encompasses:
    *   Physical Abuse
    *   Sexual Abuse
    *   Verbal and Emotional Abuse
    *   Economic Abuse

2.  Any harassment, harm, injury, or endangerment inflicted upon a woman with the intention of coercing her or any person related to her to meet any unlawful demand for dowry or any other property or valuable security.
