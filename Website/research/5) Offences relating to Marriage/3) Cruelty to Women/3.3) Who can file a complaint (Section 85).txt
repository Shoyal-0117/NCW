First Information Report (FIR) or complaint under section 220 BNS is a must to commence criminal proceedings against husband or his relatives. Such a complaint can be made by the following persons:

Person aggrieved, i.e. the married woman,
Her father, mother, brother, sister,
Father’s brother or sister,
Mother’s brother or sister,
With court’s permission, any person related to such woman by blood, marriage or adoption.
