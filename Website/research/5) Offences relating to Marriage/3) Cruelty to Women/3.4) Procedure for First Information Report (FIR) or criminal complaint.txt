

**Filing a Complaint for Cruelty (Section 85)**

To initiate criminal proceedings against a husband or his relatives for the offence of cruelty, a First Information Report (FIR) or a formal complaint under the relevant section is mandatory.

The following persons are authorized to file such a complaint:

*   The aggrieved person, i.e., the married woman herself.
*   Her father, mother, brother, or sister.
*   Her father's brother or sister.
*   Her mother's brother or sister.
*   Any other person related to the woman by blood, marriage, or adoption, provided they have obtained prior permission from the court.
