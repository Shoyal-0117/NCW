The ITPA punishes persons who engage in the following with imprisonment of a minimum of three years, extendable up to seven years along with a fine which may extend to INR 2000 (Indian Rupees Two Thousand):

Procuring or attempting to procure a person, with or without their consent for prostitution;

Inducing a person to travel to a place for the purpose of prostitution;

Taking or attempting to take a person, or causing them to be taken from one place to another for carrying on or being brought up to carry on prostitution; or

Causing or inducing a person to carry on prostitution.

If any of the abovementioned acts are committed against the will of any person, the punishment of imprisonment shall extend to fourteen years. Further, if the person who has been subjected to an offence hereunder is a child or a minor, punishment may extend to life or fourteen yearsÂ respectively.
