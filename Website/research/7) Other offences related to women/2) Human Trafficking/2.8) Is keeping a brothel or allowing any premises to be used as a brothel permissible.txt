No, the ITPA provides that any person, who keeps, manages or assists in keeping, or management of a brothel shall be punishable with imprisonment for a minimum of a year and a maximum of three years along with a fine which may extend to INR 2000 (Indian Rupees Two Thousand). Further, on subsequent offences the threshold may be increased to two to five years along with a fine which may extend to INR 2000 (Indian Rupees Two Thousand).

Apart from the above, any person who is the owner or landlord of a premises; or occupies or is in charge of and uses or permits any other person to use such premises as a brothel shall be punishable with imprisonment for a term extendable up to two years along with a fine which may extend to INR 2000 (Indian Rupees Two Thousand). Subsequent offences may be punishable with a term of five years and also with fine.
