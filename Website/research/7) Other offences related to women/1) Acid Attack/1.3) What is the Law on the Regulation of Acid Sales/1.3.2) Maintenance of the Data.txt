

**Regulations on Acid Sales**

The sale of acid over-the-counter (without a valid prescription) is strictly prohibited unless the seller adheres to specific record-keeping requirements.

The seller is mandated to maintain a detailed logbook or register that records every acid sale. This register must contain the following information:

*   The details of the person to whom the acid is sold.
*   The quantity of acid sold.
*   The address of the person buying the acid.
*   The reason for procuring the acid.
