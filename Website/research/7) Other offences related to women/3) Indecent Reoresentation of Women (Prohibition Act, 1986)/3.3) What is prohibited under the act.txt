

**Prohibited Acts**

The Act explicitly prohibits a range of activities involving the indecent representation of women. These include:

*   Publishing, or causing to be published, any advertisement that contains an indecent representation of women.
*   Arranging or taking part in the publication or exhibition of such advertisements.
*   Producing, selling, hiring, distributing, circulating, or sending any material—such as books, pamphlets, films, photographs, paintings, or slides—that contain an indecent representation of women.
