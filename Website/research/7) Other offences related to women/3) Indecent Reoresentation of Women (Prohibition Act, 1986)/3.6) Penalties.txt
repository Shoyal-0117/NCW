
**Penalties for Violation**

The penalties for offences under this Act are as follows:

*   **First Conviction:** Imprisonment for a term which may extend to two years, and a fine which may extend to two thousand rupees.
*   **Second or Subsequent Conviction:** Imprisonment for a term which shall not be less than six months but may extend to five years, and also with a fine which shall not be less than ten thousand rupees but may extend to one lakh rupees.

Any violation of this Act is a **cognizable** and **bailable** offence.
