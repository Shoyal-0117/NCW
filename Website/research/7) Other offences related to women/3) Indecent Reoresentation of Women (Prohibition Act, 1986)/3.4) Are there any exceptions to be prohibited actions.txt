

**Exceptions under the Act**

The Act provides specific exceptions for material that is deemed to be in the public interest. The following are not considered 'indecent representation of women':

*   Any material—such as a book, film, painting, or photograph—that is justified as being for the public good on the grounds of science, literature, art, or learning.
*   Any representation that is sculpted, engraved, painted, or otherwise depicted on or in any ancient monument, temple, or on a car used for conveying idols, or kept for any bona fide religious purpose.
*   Any film that has been certified for public exhibition by the competent authority under the applicable laws.
