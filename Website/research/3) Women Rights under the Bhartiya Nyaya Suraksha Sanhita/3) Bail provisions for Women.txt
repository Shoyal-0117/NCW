Section 480 of Bhartiya Nyaya Suraksha Sanhita deals with bail in case of non-bailable offences. According to it, person shall not be released on bail if:

1) There is reasonable ground to believe that he has committed an offence punishable with death or life imprisonment, or
2) He has been previously convicted for an offence punishable with death, life imprisonment, or for a term of seven years or more, or
3) He has been convicted on two or more occasions on other offences with a term between three and seven years.


Exception for Women: This Section contains certain exceptions such as the court may grant bail even in these cases, if such person is under the age of 16 or is a woman or is sick or infirm.
