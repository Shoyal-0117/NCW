
**Offence of Importing a Girl for Illicit Intercourse**

A person commits an offence under this section if they import any girl under the age of twenty-one years into India from a foreign country. The offence requires that this act is done with the intention, or with the knowledge that it is likely, that the girl will be forced or seduced into having illicit intercourse with another person.

**Punishment:**

The punishment for this offence is imprisonment for a term which may extend to ten years, and the offender shall also be liable to pay a fine.
