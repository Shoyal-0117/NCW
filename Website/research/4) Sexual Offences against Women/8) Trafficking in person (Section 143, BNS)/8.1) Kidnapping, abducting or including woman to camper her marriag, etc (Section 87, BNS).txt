

**Offence of Kidnapping or Abducting a Woman**

A person commits an offence under this section if they kidnap or abduct any woman with the intent, or with the knowledge that it is likely, that she will be:

-   Compelled to marry any person against her will, or
-   Forced or seduced into having illicit intercourse.

**Punishment:**

The punishment for this offence is imprisonment for a term which may extend to ten years, and the offender shall also be liable to pay a fine.
