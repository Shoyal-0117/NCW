**Scope of Protection under the POSH Act**

The POSH Act is designed to protect an **'aggrieved woman'** who experiences sexual harassment at the workplace. This protection extends whether the harassment is perpetrated by the employer or by any other employee.

**Definition of an 'Aggrieved Woman'**
This term encompasses:
-   Any woman who is employed at a workplace.
-   It also includes any woman, even if she is not an employee, who faces an act of sexual harassment in connection with that workplace.

**Definition of a 'Workplace'**
The Act defines 'workplace' in a very broad manner. It includes:
-   Organizations and institutions in both the government and private sectors.
-   Hospitals, sports institutes, and sports venues.
-   Any dwelling place or house.
-   Any place visited by an employee during the course of their employment, including transportation provided by the employer.
