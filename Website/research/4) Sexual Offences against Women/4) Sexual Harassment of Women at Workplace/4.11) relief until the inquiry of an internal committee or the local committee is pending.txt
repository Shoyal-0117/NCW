
An aggrieved woman has the right to request interim relief during the inquiry process. She can submit a written request to the Internal Committee or the Local Committee, which may recommend any of the following measures to the employer:

*   The transfer of either the aggrieved woman or the respondent to another workplace.
*   The grant of leave to the aggrieved woman for a period of up to three months.
*   An order restraining the respondent from reporting on the work performance of, or supervising in any capacity, the aggrieved woman.
