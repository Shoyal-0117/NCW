

If the allegations against a respondent are proven, the Internal Committee or the Local Committee may recommend that the employer or the District Officer take action. This action can include, but is not limited to:

*   Requiring a written apology from the respondent.
*   Issuing a warning, reprimand, or censure.
*   Withholding a promotion, pay raise, or increments.
*   Terminating the respondent's employment.
*   Requiring the respondent to undergo counseling sessions.
*   Requiring the respondent to perform community service.
