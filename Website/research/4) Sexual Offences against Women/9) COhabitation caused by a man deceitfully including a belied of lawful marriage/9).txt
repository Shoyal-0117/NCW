

**Section 81: Offence of Cohabitation by Deceit**

This section states that any man who, through deception, makes a woman believe she is lawfully married to him when she is not, and thereby induces her to cohabit or engage in sexual intercourse with him under this belief, is punishable under this law.

**Punishment:**
The offender shall be liable to imprisonment of either description for a term which may extend to ten years, and shall also be liable to a fine.
