
**Essentials of the Offence under Section 74, BNS (Outraging the Modesty of a Woman)**

For an act to constitute the offence of outraging the modesty of a woman under this section, the following essential conditions must be met:

1.  The victim of the assault must be a woman.
2.  The accused must have assaulted the woman or used criminal force against her.
3.  The assault or use of criminal force must have been done with the specific intention of outraging her modesty, or with the knowledge that it was likely to have that effect.
